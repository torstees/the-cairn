Welcome to The Cairn — a practice companion for traditional Irish music.

## Tune Boxes

A **TuneBox** is a named, instrument-scoped catalog of tunes you're currently
working on. Create one for each instrument you play, and add tunes to it as
you learn them.

## Practice Lists

Within a box, a **Practice List** groups tunes for a focused practice goal —
either building repertoire toward performance readiness, or woodshedding a
tune until it sticks.

## Tracking Progress

Rate your confidence after each practice session. The Cairn uses spaced
repetition to suggest what to review next, so tunes you know well come back
less often and tunes you're still learning come back sooner.
